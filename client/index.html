Crypto Cats is an NFT Marketplace where you can:

  • Buy

  • Sell

  • Collect

  • &

  • Breed

  • THESE CREEPY EYED CATS...

What's this then?

Crypto Cats is a copycat of the well known and successful NFT Marketplace called CryptoKitties. You have the opportunity to collect cats that will continually increase in rarity and value.

There are never enough cats in the world, so in addition to simply buying and selling your cats, you can also breed them to birth new cats. The little fresh faced kittens that are born have a random mix of "Cattributes" from both the mother and the father, so each cat is totally unique.
