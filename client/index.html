Crypto Cats is an NFT Marketplace where you can:

  • Buy

  • Sell

  • Collect

  • Breed

What Exactly is Crypto Cats?

Crypto Cats is a clone of the well known and successful NFT Marketplace called CryptoKitties. You have the opportunity to collect rare cats that will continually increase in rarity and value.

There are never enough cats in the world... so in addition to simply buying and selling your cats, you can also breed them to birth new cats. The little fresh faced kittens that are born have a random mix of "Cattributes" from both the mother and the father and is tradable as well as breedable, given that they are old enough of course.
